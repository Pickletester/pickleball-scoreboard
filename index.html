Our Story

Pittsburgh's Newest
Wedding Venue

Georgetown Oaks is a newly completed wedding and event venue located just outside Pittsburgh in Canonsburg, Pennsylvania. Not only do we have a stunning ballroom, but we also feature a speakeasy, outdoor patios, and a restaurant — every space designed to make your celebration unforgettable.

What Sets Us Apart

Designed for
Every Occasion

Our interior flows seamlessly into a 1,280 square foot outdoor sunset viewing deck, offering an idyllic backdrop for receptions, ceremonies, cocktail hours, or evening portraits. With a fireplace, built-in bar, and floor-to-ceiling windows, Georgetown Oaks is unlike anything else in the Pittsburgh area.
